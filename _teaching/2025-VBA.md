**Level**: Engineering Cycle  
**Hours**: 12h  
**Language**: French  
**Tools**: Excel, VBA (macros)

## Course Content
* Core Excel functions (formulas, built-in functions, referencing)
* VBA macros (recording, editing, and writing simple procedures)
* Connecting Excel functions with macros (automating calculations and workflows)
* Basic user interaction (buttons/forms to trigger macros)

## Skills Developed
Students learn to automate spreadsheet workflows by combining Excel functions with VBA macros, turning manual steps into repeatable tools.
